DODGE BALL

Press an arrow key to dodge the boxes.

Too easy?  Press SHIFT+2 to double the speed.
Too hard?  Press SHIFT+3 to halve the speed.
Press SHIFT+1 to return speed to normal.
Press number 0 to mute sound.

"Freedom is... freedom can actually be boring, you've got to realize that." Peter Molyneux
