SHADOW OF THE DRONES

You are a doctor in a village under siege by drones. 

Press an ARROW KEY to dodge the drones.

Rescue a survivor to score a point.
However, every rescue summons more drones.
The gaps between drones are moving.
Can you quickly trace the gaps?

Press number 0 to mute sound.
Too easy?  Press SHIFT+2 to double the speed.
Too hard?  Press SHIFT+3 to halve the speed.
Press SHIFT+1 to return speed to normal.

Music "Drone Strike" by SEVA
https://soundcloud.com/neurofunkgrid/seva-drone-strike?in=neurofunkgrid/sets/seva-shapeless-ep-nfg003
